about me

Hi! My name is Crystal and I am a sophomore at Cornell University studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering. I'm also minoring in Information Science, Electrical and Computer Engineering, and Visual Studies .

I originally came to Cornell intending to major in Electrical and Computer Engineering, but after my first year, I realized I loved how computer science at Cornell encompassed software, hardware, and the intersection of technology with society. I love learning about anything I can, and am always trying to discover new things.

At school, I am the business lead for CUSail, where I lead the team's branding, internal, and administrative affairs. I also lead publicity and social media for IEEE Cornell, and volunteer with Alpha Phi Omega, a national service fraternity.
